Welcome Note

Thank you for visiting!

I’m a Senior Research Scientist and co-founder at Oumi.ai (Open Universal Machine Intelligence), where we strive to make AI more open, collaborative, and responsible—ensuring equitable access and meaningful impact across science, industry, and society. (We are hiring)!

I strongly believe that AI should be accessible, transparent, and built for everyone. But true openness isn’t achieved alone—it thrives on collaboration. Whether you’re an AI researcher, developer, user, or simply curious, I encourage you to join our open-source platform and help shape AI’s democratic future, together.

I’m passionate about pushing the boundaries of AI with human-like visual and linguistic capabilities. My research focuses on deep learning for multimodal AI, leveraging natural language to capture semantic and emotional nuances in visual data.

Before Oumi, I was the Head of Applied Research at Steel Perlot, and before I worked as Research Scientist for the Creative Vision team of Snap Research.

I earned my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science Department of Stanford University conducting research with the Geometric Computing Lab under the supervision of Leo Guibas.
